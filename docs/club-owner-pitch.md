# Fescue Golf Club — Member Platform Overview

A purpose-built digital home for the club. Everything your members need, nothing they don't.

---

## What the Platform Offers Today

### Public Presence
- **Marketing website** — landing page, about page, and contact/tour request form built to reflect the club's brand and aesthetic
- **Membership request pipeline** — prospective members submit a tour request directly from the site; you manage the entire funnel from one place
- **Social sharing** — custom branded preview image when the site link is shared via text or social media
- **Content-managed copy** — update homepage and about page body text, photos, and feature callouts without touching any code, directly from the content studio

### Member Portal
- **Secure login** — invite-only access; members are onboarded via personalised email invite, not open registration
- **Bay reservations** — members self-serve book simulator bays in 60, 90, or 120 minute blocks; no phone calls, no back-and-forth
- **Guest management** — members register guests at the time of booking
- **Reservation history** — members can view and cancel their upcoming bookings from their account page
- **Social calendar** — upcoming club events displayed in a full calendar view with event details
- **Bulletin board** — club announcements and news posted by admins, pinnable to the top of the feed
- **Member directory** — browse fellow members; includes member-since year and Club Champion recognition
- **Announcement ticker** — high-visibility scrolling banner at the top of the portal for time-sensitive news (e.g. tournament start, bay closures)

### Admin Tools
- **Member management** — view all active members, deactivate accounts instantly (access revoked immediately on next request)
- **Invite system** — send, resend, and rescind member invitations by name and email
- **Membership request pipeline** — manage tour requests from pending → contacted → invited → onboarded or declined; send intro emails directly from the dashboard
- **Reservation oversight** — view all bookings by date, cancel on behalf of members
- **Bay blackout periods** — block individual bays or all bays for maintenance, events, or holidays
- **Content studio** — manage bulletin posts, social events, club champions, page copy, and the announcement banner from a clean editorial interface
- **Guest lead tracking** — guests registered at bookings are surfaced as potential membership prospects

## The Value It Brings

| Problem | How the platform solves it |
|---|---|
| Reservations managed manually | Members book themselves 24/7; admin sees everything in one view |
| No central member communication | Bulletin board, social calendar, and announcement ticker keep members informed |
| Membership inquiries handled ad hoc | Structured pipeline with email automation tracks every prospect |
| Club relies on generic tools (GroupMe, spreadsheets, Calendly) | One branded platform that does all of it, with your identity on it |
| No professional web presence | Polished public site that matches the quality of the club itself |
| Content updates require a developer | Admin can update copy, photos, events, and announcements independently |

## Built for Ownership — Not Dependency

- **You own the data.** Member records, bookings, and history live in your own database — not locked inside a third-party app.
- **No per-seat SaaS fees.** Infrastructure costs are a flat monthly hosting bill, not a per-member or per-booking charge.
- **Invite-only by design.** The exclusivity of the club is reflected in how members get access.

## Future Improvements

### Near-term
- **Merch store** — branded club merchandise via Shopify, accessible directly from the member portal
- **Mobile-optimised experience** — progressive refinement for booking and calendar on phones
- **Email notifications** — booking confirmations, upcoming event reminders, bulletin digests

### Medium-term
- **Tournament management** — bracket creation, leaderboards, and signup flows built into the calendar
- **Member dues / billing** — integrated payment collection for annual or monthly membership fees
- **Waitlist management** — automated waitlist for fully-booked time slots with member notification on cancellation
- **Push notifications** — opt-in browser notifications for announcements and booking reminders

### Longer-term
- **Handicap tracking** — members log rounds; club-wide leaderboard and handicap index
- **Analytics dashboard** — bay utilisation rates, peak booking hours, member engagement metrics
- **Native mobile app** — iOS/Android wrapper for the full portal experience
